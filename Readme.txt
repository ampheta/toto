Alpha version of Chrome Browser extension to reveal the names and faces of people tracking your online browsing. Originally developed for the 2013 Ghostery "Hack the Trackers" hackathon in New York. This software is released under the GPLv3.

Team Members: Gustavo Faria, Fei Liu, Scott Powell

TO INSTALL:  Go to the project page on Github, https://github.com/dakong27/toto, and click the "Download ZIP" button in the right-hand margin.  Unzip the file on your desktop or other folder of your choice.  Open the Chrome browser and go to Tools->Settings->Extensions and click "Load unpacked extension" to select the location where you unzipped Toto.  Restart the browser and you should be ready to roll!
